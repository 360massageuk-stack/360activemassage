Relaxation Massage in Edinburgh

If you feel tired, tense or simply need some time away from the demands of everyday life, I would be delighted to welcome you for a calm and unhurried relaxation massage in Edinburgh.

Every treatment is centred around what you need on the day. You may want to switch off completely, enjoy warm oil and flowing massage techniques, or combine relaxation with slightly firmer work around areas such as the shoulders, neck, back or legs.

A massage designed around you

I do not believe in giving every client the same routine. Before your massage begins, we will have a quick conversation about how you are feeling, any areas of tension and the type of pressure you enjoy.

Some clients prefer a very gentle and soothing treatment. Others like a little more pressure while still keeping the massage relaxing. I will adapt the treatment throughout so that you can feel comfortable, supported and able to properly unwind.

How relaxation massage may help

  • Creates time to slow down and switch off
  • Helps ease everyday stress and muscular tension
  • May support more restful sleep
  • Can soothe tight shoulders, neck, back and legs
  • Encourages a sense of physical and mental reset
  • Can be adapted from gentle to moderately firm pressure

Relaxing does not have to mean ineffective

A relaxation massage can feel deeply calming while still paying attention to the areas that need it most. I may use flowing hand and forearm techniques, warm oil and gentle mobility where appropriate, always working within your comfort.

My aim is not to rush through a fixed sequence. I want you to feel that the treatment has been created for you, with enough time to settle, breathe and let the tension begin to ease.

Your comfort comes first

As a male massage therapist, I understand that trust, reassurance and professionalism matter, particularly if it is your first appointment. I will explain anything you are unsure about, respect your privacy and make sure you feel comfortable throughout.

You can ask for the pressure to be changed at any point. This is your treatment, and you should never feel that you have to tolerate anything uncomfortable.

Massage at The Pleasance, Edinburgh

I work from a welcoming treatment space at The Pleasance in Edinburgh. The room is kept warm and comfortable, creating a peaceful environment where you can step away from the outside world for a while.

Selected mobile massage appointments are also available when having treatment in your own space would suit you better.

Is relaxation massage right for you?

Relaxation massage may be a good choice if you feel overworked, mentally drained, physically tense or simply ready for some proper time to yourself.

Whether this is your first massage or something you enjoy regularly, my goal is simple: to help you leave feeling calmer, looser, more comfortable and genuinely better than when you arrived.
