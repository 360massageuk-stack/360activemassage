About

Massage that's built around your body

Looking for male massage in Edinburgh? Your treatment should feel personal, not clinical. Every session is shaped around you, your body, your lifestyle, and what you actually need on the day. I don't follow a scripted routine or rushed "standard" treatment. I'm constantly refining my approach to make sure each session feels tailored, effective, and genuine.

My background comes from years of active training and competitive sport, so I understand first hand what recovery, tension, fatigue, and physical strain really feel like. That experience naturally influences the way I work, combining knowledge, intuition, and a practical understanding of the body.

Based in The Pleasance, Edinburgh, I work from a private, comfortable setting in one of the city's most central locations. Mobile visits are also available across Edinburgh and Scotland.

Treatments & Specialisms

Every session is tailored. Duration and approach adapted to what your body needs on the day.

01
Relaxation Massage

Your official off switch. Long, soothing strokes, gentle kneading, and zero rush. All about melting stress, boosting circulation, and leaving you blissed out. Most people fall asleep. I take it as a compliment.

02
Deep Tissue Massage

Targeted therapy for chronic tension, stubborn knots, and restricted movement. Slow, deliberate pressure into the deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue, releasing adhesions and restoring flexibility.

03
Tailor Made Massage

Every session starts with a conversation about pressure, focus areas, how deep or relaxing you want it. I build the whole session around your preferences. No cookie-cutter routines. No laminated menu. Real results.

04
Sports Massage

Tailored for athletes and the active. Relieves muscle tension, enhances flexibility, and accelerates recovery. Whether preparing for competition or recovering from an intensive training block.

05
Couples Massage

I come to you — your home, your space, your evening — and deliver two simultaneous, fully tailored massages side by side. No spa ing, no travel. A genuinely luxurious experience for two. Perfect for anniversaries, date nights, or just because.

06
Body Scrub

A rejuvenating full-body scrub paired with a soothing massage, finished with a warm shower rinse. Silky smooth skin, deeply refreshed — the kind of treatment that leaves you feeling entirely renewed from the outside in.

07
Reflexology

Precise pressure to specific points on the feet, hands, or ears, each corresponding to organs and systems throughout the body. Promotes deep relaxation and supports natural healing in ways that go far beyond the feet alone.

08
Indian Head Massage

Targets tension in the scalp, neck, and shoulders — where stress quietly accumulates. Relieves headaches, mental fatigue, and muscle tightness. Deeply calming. Ideal for those who carry the world in their shoulders.

09
Infrared Recovery & Relaxation

The soothing warmth of an infrared sauna blanket combined with a relaxing head, scalp, neck and shoulder massage. Ease tension, encourage deep relaxation. Shower facilities available after treatment.

10
Aloe Vera Oil Massage

A cooling, skin-soothing treatment using aloe vera oil. Ideal during warmer months or after sun exposure. Hydrating, calming, and deeply relaxing — with the same tailored pressure and attention as every 360 session.

11
Mobility & Stretch Therapy

Designed to loosen tight areas, improve flexibility, and get your body moving properly again. A mix of assisted stretching and targeted release work that reduces stiffness and improves range of motion.

Questions

Frequently asked

What should I expect from my first session?

We start with a short, friendly chat about what you're after: pressure preference, focus areas, any aches or injuries to be aware of. From there I tailor the session entirely to you. There's no rigid routine.

How much pressure is used?

That's entirely up to you. I work from firm to feather-light and everything in between. You're encouraged to communicate throughout and I can adjust at any point during the session.

What do I need for a mobile session?

Just a reasonably clear space. I bring everything — a professional massage table, fresh linens, and all oils and equipment. A room roughly 3m x 2m is ideal.

Do you offer massage during the Edinburgh Fringe?

Yes — August is my busiest month and I'm available throughout the Fringe at my treatment room in The Pleasance. I see performers, crew, and visitors alike. Slots fill fast so I'd recommend ing before July.

How do I know which treatment to choose?

Not sure? Just say so. I'll have a quick chat about what you're looking for and recommend the right treatment. Or simply opt for the Tailor Made session and I'll build the perfect treatment on the day.

How far in advance should I ?

As early as possible is always best, especially for evenings and weekends. During the Edinburgh Fringe in August, availability goes quickly. That said, last minute sessions can sometimes be accommodated.

Is there a cancellation policy?

I just ask for as much notice as possible if you need to cancel or rearrange. Get in touch via WhatsApp or phone and I'll always do my best to find a solution that works for you.

I'm a Fringe performer — can you help with performance recovery?

Absolutely. Performing multiple shows daily is physically and mentally demanding. Sports massage, deep tissue work, and mobility therapy are all brilliant for keeping you in shape throughout a long run. Many performers a regular slot for the duration of the festival.
