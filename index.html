About

Massage that's built around your body

If you've had your fair share of massages, you'll clock the difference in about 30 seconds. I don't do the autopilot routine. I'm constantly tweaking and upgrading my techniques so your session is actually built around your body, not some laminated menu.

My approach is shaped by decades of competitive sport and active training. That first-hand experience of pushing the body hard, knowing what real recovery actually feels like, is what separates what I do from your average massage. I'm not just working from a textbook. I know bodies because I've lived in one that's been through it.

I'm based in The Pleasance, Edinburgh, one of the city's most central and well-connected areas. Sessions take place in a private, comfortable setting. I also offer mobile visits across Edinburgh and Scotland for those who prefer treatment at home.

What I Offer

Treatments &
Specialisms

Every session I do is tailored. Duration and approach adapted to what your body needs on the day.

01

Relaxation Massage

Your official off switch. Long, soothing strokes, gentle kneading, and zero rush. All about melting stress, boosting circulation, and leaving you blissed out. I dial it exactly to your vibe. Most people fall asleep. I take it as a compliment.

02

Deep Tissue Massage

Targeted therapy for chronic tension, stubborn knots, and restricted movement. Slow, deliberate pressure into the deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue, releasing adhesions, restoring flexibility, and reducing pain at its source.

03

Sports Massage

Tailored for athletes and the active. Relieves muscle tension, enhances flexibility, and accelerates recovery. Whether preparing for competition or recovering from an intensive training block, it's built to optimise performance and reduce injury risk.

04

Tailor Made Massage

Every session starts with a conversation about pressure, focus areas, how deep or relaxing you want it. I build the whole session around your preferences. No cookie-cutter routines. No laminated menu. Real results, real relaxation.

05

Body Scrub

A rejuvenating full-body scrub paired with a soothing massage, finished with a warm shower rinse. The result is silky smooth skin, deeply refreshed, the kind of treatment that leaves you feeling entirely renewed from the outside in.

06

Reflexology

Precise pressure to specific points on the feet, hands, or ears, each corresponding to organs and systems throughout the body. Promotes deep relaxation, improves circulation, and supports natural healing in ways that go far beyond the feet alone.

07

Indian Head Massage

Targets tension in the scalp, neck, and shoulders, where stress quietly accumulates. Relieves headaches, mental fatigue, and muscle tightness while improving circulation. Deeply calming. Ideal for those who carry the world in their shoulders.

08

Thai Healing Massage

Often called "lazy person's yoga." Rhythmic compressions, acupressure along energy lines, and gentle passive stretches combine to improve flexibility, release deep tension, and ease stiffness. Performed fully clothed. An extraordinary sense of openness follows.

09

Mobility & Stretch Therapy

Designed to loosen tight areas, improve flexibility, and get your body moving properly again. A mix of assisted stretching and targeted release work that helps reduce stiffness, improve range of motion, and leave you feeling more open, mobile, and comfortable.

10

Couples Massage

A shared experience like no other. I come to you, your home, your space, your evening, and deliver two simultaneous, fully tailored massages side by side. No spa booking, no travel, no awkward robes. Just a genuinely luxurious experience for two, in the comfort of your own surroundings. Perfect for date nights, anniversaries, birthdays, or just because. This is the treatment people remember.

Questions

Frequently asked

What should I expect from my first session?
We start with a short, friendly chat about what you're after: pressure preference, focus areas, any aches or injuries to be aware of. From there I tailor the session entirely to you. There's no rigid routine.
How much pressure is used?
That's entirely up to you. I work from firm to feather-light and everything in between. You're encouraged to communicate throughout and I can adjust at any point during the session.
What do I need for a mobile session?
Just a reasonably clear space. I bring everything including a professional massage table, fresh linens, and all oils and equipment. A room roughly 3m x 2m is ideal.
Do you offer massage during the Edinburgh Fringe?
Yes — August is my busiest month and I'm available throughout the Fringe at my treatment room in The Pleasance. I see performers, crew, and visitors alike. Slots fill fast so I'd recommend booking before July if you want a specific date.
How do I know which treatment to choose?
Not sure? Just say so. I'll have a quick chat about what you're looking for and recommend the right treatment. Or simply opt for the Tailor Made session and I'll build the perfect treatment on the day.
How far in advance should I book?
As early as possible is always best, especially for evenings and weekends. During the Edinburgh Fringe in August, availability goes quickly — I'd strongly recommend booking well in advance for that period. That said, last minute sessions can sometimes be accommodated and it's always worth getting in touch.
Is there a cancellation policy?
I just ask for as much notice as possible if you need to cancel or rearrange. Get in touch via WhatsApp or phone and I'll always do my best to find a solution that works for you.
I'm a Fringe performer — can you help with performance recovery?
Absolutely. Performing multiple shows daily is physically and mentally demanding. Sports massage, deep tissue work, and mobility therapy are all brilliant for keeping you in shape throughout a long run. Many performers book a regular slot for the duration of the festival. Get in touch to discuss what would work best for your schedule.
