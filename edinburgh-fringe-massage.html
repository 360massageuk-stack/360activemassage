Recovery during the Fringe

Massage for performers, workers and visitors

The Edinburgh Festival Fringe can mean long days, late nights, repeated performances, standing for hours and walking across the city. A professional massage can help relieve muscular tension, tired legs, back discomfort and general fatigue.

Every session at 360 Active Massage is adapted to your preferred pressure, comfort and treatment goals. Appointments take place near the Pleasance, making the studio particularly convenient for performers and visitors in Edinburgh’s Old Town and Southside.
